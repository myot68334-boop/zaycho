# ZayCho Screenshot Pack

Use these screens for both App Store and Google Play listing assets.

## Suggested capture order

1. Home screen with hero, categories, and featured products
2. Product detail screen with price, category, and add-to-cart action
3. Wishlist screen
4. Shopping cart screen
5. Assistant screen with response state

## Suggested captions

1. Browse Myanmar grocery favorites in seconds
2. Open each item for detail, pricing, and quick actions
3. Save products to wishlist for later
4. Prepare your cart before checkout
5. Ask the assistant to help you find products faster

## App Store note

Apple currently accepts high-resolution screenshots by device class. Check the official screenshot specs before final upload:

https://developer.apple.com/help/app-store-connect/reference/app-information/screenshot-specifications

## Google Play note

Google Play requires at least two screenshots to publish a store listing, and recommends at least four high-resolution screenshots for better featuring eligibility:

https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/1078870
