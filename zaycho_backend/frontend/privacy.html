Privacy Policy

ZayCho is a storefront app for browsing Myanmar grocery products, saving wishlist items, and preparing shopping orders.

What we collect

The current version stores cart items, wishlist items, and message history locally in your browser for product experience continuity. No account registration is required in this demo release.

How data is used

Product search requests and assistant prompts are used only to serve the storefront experience. This project does not currently include payment processing or user account management.

Sharing

ZayCho does not sell personal information. If analytics, payments, or account features are added in future releases, this page should be updated before store submission.
