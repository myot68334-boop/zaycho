Privacy Policy

Lyra Shop is a fashion and beauty e-commerce application with account login, catalog browsing, shopping cart, checkout, delivery tracking, and admin management tools.

Data we collect

We collect account details such as name and email, order details such as delivery address, phone number, cart contents, payment method, and delivery tracking updates. Product images uploaded by administrators are stored on the application server.

Payments

If Stripe card payments are enabled, payment details are processed by Stripe. Lyra Shop stores payment intent references and payment status, but should not store raw card details on its server.

How data is used

User data is used for authentication, order fulfillment, payment reconciliation, customer support, and delivery tracking. Admin-only tools use uploaded content to manage catalog listings.

Sharing and retention

Personal data should only be shared with service providers required for payments, hosting, and delivery. Account deletion removes the account, sessions, and stored order records from this starter application. In a production deployment, consult legal counsel to define the correct retention period for tax and shipping records.

Your choices

You can request account deletion from inside the app or by visiting the account deletion page linked below.
