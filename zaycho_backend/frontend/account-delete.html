Delete Your Account

You can delete your Lyra Shop account from inside the app after logging in by pressing the Delete Account button in the account panel.

This starter implementation deletes the user account, active sessions, and stored order records. In production, make sure your final policy clearly explains any records you must retain for legal, tax, fraud, or fulfillment reasons.
